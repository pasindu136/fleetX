Our Story

FROM AN IDEA TO SRI LANKA'S FINEST

FleetX was born out of frustration. Our founders experienced firsthand the unreliable, overpriced, and unsafe nature of traditional taxi services across the island. In 2023, they set out to change that — permanently.

Starting with just 12 vehicles in Colombo, we have grown to a nationwide fleet serving thousands of passengers every day — from budget commuters to corporate executives.
